Looking to start a business in Indonesia?.

  • Let us navigate you to Indonesia business landscape, Indonesia now becomes the most attractive destination for business expansion with specific reasons: (1) Large and growing market: Indonesia has the fourth-largest population in the world, with more than 270 million people. Its middle class is also growing, and there is a rising consumer demand for goods and services. (2) Natural resources: Indonesia is rich in natural resources such as oil, gas, coal, minerals, and timber, making it an attractive location for resource extraction and processing industries. (3) Strategic location: Indonesia is in Southeast Asia, with access to major shipping lanes and proximity to other emerging markets such as China, India, and ASEAN countries. (4) Government incentives: The Indonesian government offers various tax incentives and other benefits to foreign investors, such as tax holidays, import duty exemptions, and simplified regulations for certain industries. (5) Economic stability: Indonesia has maintained steady economic growth over the past decade, with a projected growth rate of around 5% in 2022. The government has also made efforts to improve infrastructure, education, and healthcare, which can further support business development. Overall, Indonesia's large market, abundant natural resources, strategic location, government incentives, and economic stability make it a compelling destination for foreign investment.
